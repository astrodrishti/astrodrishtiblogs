Astrology and Engineering simultaneously

Abhishek Sharma

july 2020

So first I want to ask a question that what comes in your mind when someone tells you about his/her profession. The answer is simple , an image related to work done in that particular profession. But what if i say an astrologer, the image is of a pandit ji doing pooja and if i say an engineer, the image is of a person going to work in formal shirt and trousers. But the interesting thing is that the developers of this app are engineers and astrologers simultaneously.

Being one of the developer of this project with my co-developer we are one of this kind , started engineering in 2018, my co-developer and friend being from a pandit family developed interest in Astrology . One day he told me that he can read Kundli i.e. kind of a birth chart. At that time i was like bro what are you doing and started laughing, also the chart seems to me like a whole different language so actually i was like, i dont want to go in this stuff. But then he asked for DOB, birth time and all, that was not a problem for me so i gave him and next day he was telling me about my past life and things were actually matching the way i lived. The guy met me in engineering so how could he know that much, I was shocked and that was the thing which developed my interest in astrology.

After that he also told me about my future, like career, health, wealth, etc and all the things he was telling me made me more and more happier which was 2nd reason I want to learn more about astrology. He started me telling about lagna first and what is influence of it in our life. I still remember we used to discuss all these things while travelling in local buses from our college to home. There were a lot of weird reactions from people in buses, that these guys are showing interest in such a mature study done by mostly Pandits but we never cared about that and used to discuss a lot. I daily tried to gain more and more knowledge of all these things and used to diagnose kundlis of random students from college and in 10-15 days i was like that i can predict , not very deeply but yes now i know what is this weird looking chart.

“I believe in a lot of astrology. I believe in aliens…I look up into the stars and I imagine: ‘How self-important are we to think that we are the only life-form?”

After that I sarted loosing my interest in all these stuff as i started learning a new programming knowledge, but my friend, he was day by day becoming more expert in this field and great thing was that he never took a single penny from anyone to see his birth chart as taken by many astrologers, he did all this for practice. But we both marely talked about astrology in that month.

Lockdown The Game Changer!

The game changed when Lockdown started, it was like a very good chance for CSE students to learn. So I started learning different languages and after a month time made me learn about python as a programming language and flutter for mobile App development. After a while my friend asked me to make an app of kundli to which I merely showed interest and said there are many others but he then told me to make an app which can also read kundli with a single tap. The idea suddenly striked my mind and i started working on it taking idea as a good project to practice, but ended up making an app which can actually read and check planets and there positions influencing users life with which I started adding more features into it.

While making that app I again studied about astrology and again same things happened, we started discussing more and more on chat apps and now whether I am not as good as my friend but can see birth chart and how planets are influencing person's life. At last I want to say that astrology is nothing but reality.Some people dont show interest but fact is that "यह सत्य है".
