Why can't we see God?

Abhishek Sharma

August 2020

In this modern era we believe in science, technology, etc. But when we are not able to complete a work, or I can say we want the luck in our favour we just simply say "PLEASE HELP ME GOD". Now here we are asking an entity for help which we have never seen, but we believe. Now question can be simply answered that GOD is nothing but a belief and belief can never be seen, it can only be felt. Now for making this article more interesting we can discuss about what type of belief is this or how it is developed, what is basis of this all, how majority of people believe in GOD even without a single proof. Lets Start..

See I believe in "KARMA" but we can relate this word with GOD belief. Whenever our parents or grandparents talk about GOD, they usually tells us good stories , how they saved people from bad persons, how they spread love so that everyone becomes a good person, how they used to to charities for poor ones, their friendships like "Krishna and Sudama", 5 Pandavas against a full army, Ramayana, Mahabharata, etc. All this is indicating that person should be good from his heart, he should never betray someone, help poor, etc to make this world a better place to live.

Also this is indicating when a person shows his bad side, he/she earns bad "KARMAS" which he/she has to pay in future. All this is said to us about GOD that he always helps a person who helps others and punishes one doing wrong thing. See how "KARMA" and belief of GOD is the same thing.

“God is like gravity. You don't have to believe in it for him to be in your life.”

One more theory says that GOD is in every thing, but again question comes that how to prove this also. Reason behind the statement is to add values in people about how to treat others. They must be treated in a good way keeping in mind that he/she maybe some other avatar of GOD. This one statement was also the reason for whole Ramayana. In early times giving biksha to sadhus was counted as a very good deed, but with increasing crime this belief of people is fading away and they usually tend to ignore these type of help these days.

"So whether there is God ? or it's just a belief."

Now the answer of this question can vary from person to person. Some believe in GOD, some believe in GOD only and few do not believe in anything. But at the end I want to say whether GOD exists or not we should follow the path taught by them as the things told in our sacred books are not harmful for humanity at all, because they will ultimately lead us in a good direction towards success only.
