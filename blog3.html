Bad Planetary Combinations in your Birthchart ?

Vaibhav Gautam

july 2020

Hello guys, I know a lot of people think that if you have bad combinations in your chart then no matter what you do, you'll never be able to accomplish your goals in your life and end up in trouble. This thinking is not right as a majority of people have some sort of bad planetary combinations and positions in their Birth Chart. I have seen various people in my life who have performed splendidly in their life despite having some bad combinations.

I am pretty sure that if you're reading this then your life will surely change. Have you heard about the butterfly effect ? In simple words, even a small decision or action you take can have a really deep or massive consequence(s) in the future and if you're reading this then maybe in the future it will have a huge impact on your like. Who knows right ?

To predict the future is not an easy task and trust me no one can actually predict the future with 100 percent accuracy. Our great sages had extraordinary knowledge and they were very learned. In Kali Yuga much of the knowledge that we once had is lost. But still we have managed to learn a little bit of astrology which I believe is enough to make sure that we walk on the right path and reach our destination with ease.

“Astrology had an important role in the ancient world. You can’t understand many things unless you know something about astrology—the plays of Shakespeare and so on.”

So even if you have bad combinations, don't worry there must be some other good combinations nullifying the bad results. Doing what you love is the key to your success and also praying and making the gods happy isn't a tough task.Even if you don't believe in god (I am certain you do), meditating is always a relaxing and stressbusting activity. Praying is nothing but a sort of meditation.

The Mantras!

They always create a positive aura around you and spread positive vibes. So don't worry if you have bad planetary combinations, just work on your flaws and take extra precaution during the Mahadsha aur Antardasha of the planet that is not well placed in your horoscope and worship it's ruling deity. In most of the cases sheer willpower can fix all the bad aspects of your kundli. I wish you success and happiness in your life.
